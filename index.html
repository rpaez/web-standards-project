Site Intent

The client, Burt’s Bees wants to create a website for a new lifestyle brand called Burt’s Bees Baby. The intent of this website would be to provide a digital commerce portal that allows users to buy signature Burt’s Bees Baby apparel and health care products online as well as provide parents an area to discuss and share how these items benefit the child.

Phase one of this new brand would to simply gain a foothold and being marketing the new lifestyle brand to parents. Only select items will be available for sale during phase-one of the operation with phase-two only being implemented if phase-one deems to be successful. It should be noted that the client has only signed a contract for phase-one implementation.

New brand will leverage current Burt’s Bee’s wireframe to speed developments time. Only core templates will be used, the new site design will be wrapped around this template. New design should be “softer” than the Burt’s Bee’s branded website.

The Burt’s Bees Baby brand will be simple in respect to phase-two as it will only have a landing page to promote products with a section displaying the latest from blog posts, an online “shop” and a contact section.

Competitive Analysis

The Burt’s Bees brand carries an “organic” and “safe” image, which the client intends to use to its advantage when trying to acquire new business. The Burt’s Bees Baby line of, both apparel and hygiene, products are grown and processed using earth friendly means. After researching it’s core competition we agreed that are in a decent position to precede with confidence as long as we purchase key “keywords” from search engines and have a strategic focus on a SEO and PPC initiatives with a continuing review of possible affiliate programs.

Residential Demographics:

  • Mothers and/or fathers from 25 – 45 years of age having some college education.
  • Average family income between 95K – 250K
  • Families in or near urban communities with “green” initiatives (i.e. community gardens)

Target Audience

The Burt’s Bees Baby target audience is new parents with a personal interest in being “green” for both the health of their baby(s) and the planet. They tend to live and work in urban communities that have begun “green” initiatives such as community gardens, car pools, bicycles available to residents, electric car charging stations and any other community based initiative to reduce pollution and being clearing a path to a cleaner and “greener” future. This audience is or has some college education and in most cases one of the parents is college educated. They like to live a natural life style and have the urge to only purchase items, even at higher cost, that are safer for them and the environment.
